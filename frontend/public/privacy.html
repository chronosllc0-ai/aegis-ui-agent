Privacy Policy Overview

Aegis stores operational data needed to deliver agent functionality, including account details, session settings, and conversation records tied to your user identity. Data is segmented by account and role, and administrative operations are audited to support governance and security review.

When tools interact with external services, only the minimum required credentials and metadata are retained for continuity and troubleshooting. Users can request deletion, export, or support assistance through official contact channels.

See the full legal policy in the in-app privacy document for binding terms.
